Combat


Combat is very straght forward in Kal-Rul:
All combat is turn based, where you take a turn to preform an action, then the enemy does, repeat until someone is dead.

There's a couple different things you can do in combat, First you can attack using your equipped weapon, Second being attempting to run, you can't always get away though, and sometimes take some damage. You can also bring items into combat, and use them, for more details on equipping items for combat you can look at the Inventory Tutorial. You can also bring skills into combat, for more information about skills you can see the Skills Tutorial.

After combat is complete and you defeat your opponent, your loot, as well as any experence you have earned. Eventually you will level up! atuomaticlly upping a few of your stats, and giving you some free points to allocate at your own choosing, plus one skill point to spend on the skills tab!
