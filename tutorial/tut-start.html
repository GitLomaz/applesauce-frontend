Getting Started


NOTE: This page assumes you've read the other pages regarding the user interface

Getting started off in a game can be difficult, it's easy to end up somwhere you shouldn't be, and quickly get yourself killed, so here's a couple starting pointers:

  • You have a bone you found in your inventory that would make a nice club
  • The farmer to the north could use a hand with his crops, you should talk to him
  • Don't venture outside the town until you feel you're ready, keep note of the zone level in the information panel to know what to expect
  • Once you have some basic gear, there is a cave to the north east with slightly harder enemies, as well as a couple quests

Good Luck!!
