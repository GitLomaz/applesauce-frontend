Skills Screen


The skills page is divided into three different sections, each with their own purpose, here we will be taking a glance at what each tab does, and how/when to use it.
The first tab is the Allocate Skills tab, where you are able to unlock skills by assigning points to them, to do this click the skill you want to (assuming it's unlocked) to assign the points.. under the skill you can see markers that show the current skill level, how many unconfirmed points are in it, and the maximum level of the skill.

Once the points have been allocated, you need to confirm selections by clicking the CONFIRM button at the bottom. NOTE: once a point is confirmed, it can not be changed, and changing tabs while points have been allocated but not confirmed will cause the changes to be reverted.

The Second tab is used for using your skills while not in combat, to do this double click on the skill, note that some skills can't be used outside of combat (combat only skills) or are always activated (passive skills). When attempting to use the skill, if it can not be used you will recieve a warning saying why where the result normally appears.

To use a skill in combat, the skill must be equipped, the third and final tab is where this can be done. At any time you can equip up to 4 different skills, to do this all you need to do is double click a skill on the tree to move it to your combat bar. To remove a combat skill from your bar simplly double click on the skill on the top combat bar.
